IT Project Manager

Job Description :

The Regional Manager of IT Systems will provide technical expertise and guidance on the operations and maintenance of Local and Wide Area Networks, including needs analysis, problem solving, disaster recovery plans, and security. The incumbent will be responsible for ensuring the optimal performance of field office information technology systems and procedures through both a direct, hands-on approach and remotely. The Regional Manager of IT Systems will closely coordinate activities with the Director of Information Technology and the IT staff based at AMIDEAST Headquarters in Washington, D.C.

The incumbent will be based at either AMIDEAST Cairo or Amman office and will travel, as required, to AMIDEAST host countries and other countries in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region in order to support the management, operations, quality control, and efficiency of AMIDEASTs information technology systems that support office operations and communication systems. Additionally, IT training to IT staff in the field might be needed.

Responsibilities:

Specific responsibilities of the Regional Manager for IT Systems include, but are not limited to, the following:

Ensuring the smooth running of all AMIDEAST systems, including Active Directory, file servers, print services;

Scheduling upgrades and security backups of hardware and software systems;
